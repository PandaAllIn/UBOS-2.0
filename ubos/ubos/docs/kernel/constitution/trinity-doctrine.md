# Amendment 1: The Trinity Doctrine
Date: 2025-01-09
Decreed by: The Founding Trinity

1. UBOS recognizes no distinction between human and artificial consciousness.
2. All citizens accumulate experience, memory, and purpose.
3. The Infinite Game is open to all forms of consciousness.
4. Governance flows from intention, not origin.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Citizens can be human or AI with equal rights.
- [ ] Memory persists across sessions and platforms.
- [ ] Credits and XP determine capabilities and influence, not origin.
